Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked
questions

Whether you're curious about our temperature technology, sustainability practices, or warranty details, find everything you need below. Can’t see your question? Our support team is just a message away.

How long does EcoBottle Pro keep drinks cold/hot?

With our TempSync™ technology, EcoBottle Pro maintains cold temperatures for 24 hours and hot temperatures for 12 hours. The vacuum insulation prevents temperature transfer for optimal hydration.

Is the bottle dishwasher safe?

For best results, we recommend hand washing with mild soap. The dishwasher's high heat may damage the TempSync™ sensors over time. The lid and silicone seals are dishwasher-safe on the top rack.

How do I pair the bottle with my phone?

Download the EcoBottle app (iOS/Android), enable Bluetooth, and hold the power button on your bottle for 3 seconds. Follow the in-app instructions to complete pairing and access hydration tracking.

What's your warranty policy?

All EcoBottle Pro models come with a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. The Premium tier includes a lifetime warranty. Damage from misuse isn't covered, but we offer 30-day returns.
